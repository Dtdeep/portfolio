About Me

My name is Desthiny T. Dumagan, a second-year Bachelor of Science in Information Technology student, Section B. I grew up in Pantukan, Davao de Oro (formerly Compostela Valley), a place known for its beach resorts and rapid development. I am proficient in JavaScript and C#, and I have a strong passion for web and application development — not just as an academic pursuit, but as something I actively explore on my own time. My short-term goal is to design, deploy, and maintain high-quality, scalable full-stack websites to build a strong portfolio. I value kindness to myself, honesty, and a deep dedication to mastering my chosen field.
